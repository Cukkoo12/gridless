  # Gridless Inventory
  *Say goodbye to boring slots! Place your items freely, pixel by pixel.*
</div>

---

**Gridless** completely removes the restrictive 9x3 grid from the player inventory, chests, and other storage screens. You can now place your items anywhere you want in a seamless, grid-free space. 

### ✨ Features
- **Free Placement:** Drop your items anywhere on the screen.
- **Auto-Sort & Merge (Press R):** Press `R` to instantly merge partial stacks and organize your items into neat, perfect rows. Identical items stack perfectly on top of each other!
- **Drag & Paint:** Hold Left-Click or Right-Click and drag your mouse to "paint" and place items perfectly across the screen!
- **Quick Drop & Hotbar:** Hover over any item and press `Q` to drop it into the world, or press `1-9` to instantly swap it with your hotbar slots.
- **Smart Magnet (Double Click):** Double click on an item to gather all identical items nearby into your hand.
- **Shift-Click to Chest:** Shift-Click items in your gridless area to instantly send them to an open chest, furnace, or your hotbar.
- **Universal Support:** Works beautifully with player inventories, chests, furnaces, and creative mode!
- **No Data Loss:** Item coordinates are saved as NBT data, so your inventory layout is always preserved.

## 📥 Installation

1. Make sure you have [Fabric Loader](https://fabricmc.net/use/installer/) installed.
2. Download the [Fabric API](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api) mod and put it in your `.minecraft/mods` folder.
3. Download the `.jar` file of this mod and put it in your `.minecraft/mods` folder.
4. Launch the game and enjoy the freedom of your inventory!
